PROJECT 1: VIDEO AS CULTURAL CRITIQUE

“PURPOSE”, Digital Video, 2:01:00
A comparison between organic materials and re-purposed objects in society. When juxtaposed, the concept of purpose arises.





PROJECT 2: VIDEO AS INSTALLATION

“Empty Vs. Busy”, Digital Video Installation, 2:30:00
This piece explores daily life, not only as a whole community but also as an individual. Concepts such as hustle & bustle, serenity, desolation, individualism, and conformity are apparent all at once. What makes one Empty? What makes one Busy? Reflect.




PROJECT 3: CRITICAL INTERACTIVITY

“What Makes Me Happy”, Interactive Website
An interactive website piece that reflects on self. “AUTO”, “DESIGN”, “SKATE”, “BIKE”, and “RUN” being key elements that directly translate to happiness. What makes YOU happy?
